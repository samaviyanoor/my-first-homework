top 5 action animes

The anime industry spans a variety of genres, but arguably, the action genre is the the most popular
and prominent of them all. By now, action anime has developed a strong appeal among all demographics,
not just young boys, making animes like "bleach" and "my hero academia" highly mainstream and popular.

Best Action Anime According to IMDB

Here we will rank animes from 5 to 1 according to a well known website, IMDB.

1. Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood

[ 2009 - 2010 ]

"Two brothers search for a philsopher's stone after an attempt to revive
their deceased mother goes awry and leaves them in damaged physical
forms."

Rated: 9.1

Creator: Hiromu Arakawa
Stars: Kent Williams, Lemasa Kayumi, Matthew Leonhart
Episodes: 68 ep

2. Attack on Titan

[ 2013 - 2023 ]

"After his hometown is destroyed, young Eren Jeager vows to cleanse
the earth of the giant humanoid titans that have brought humanity
to the brick of extention."

Rated: 9.1

Creator: Isayama
Stars: Jessie James, Grelle Bryce, Trina Nishimura
Episodes: 98 ep

3. Hunter x Hunter

[ 2011 - 2014 ]

"Gon aspires to become a hunter, an exceptional being capable of greatness.
With his friends and his potential, he seeks out his father, who left
him when he was younger."

Rated: 9.0

Creator: Yoshiro Togashi
Stars: Issei Futamata, Megumi Han, Cristina Valenzuala
Episodes: 148 ep

4. Legend of the Galactic heroes

[ 1988 - 1997 ]

"Various people, especially two rising commanders, cope with a massive

Rated: 9.0

Creator: Katsumi Michihara
Stars: Yusaka Yara, Ryo horikawa, Katsuji Mori

[ 1998 - 1999 ]
